Hello! I'm Dayton. Most days, you’ll find me deep in VS or refining gameplay mechanics, all driven by a goal to create experiences that people truly love to play.

My journey started back when I was a kid, constantly trying to keep up with my sister in whatever game we were playing. Those hours on the infront of the monitor didn’t just make me a gamer but they also sparked a curiosity about how those worlds were built and I haven’t stopped thinking since.

When I’m not behind a monitor, I’m usually chasing a different kind of precision or immersion. Whether I’m focusing on a target at the archery range or exploring a reef while scuba diving, I love the chill and relaxation that comes with outdoor adventures. I try to bring that same sense of focus and discovery back to my work in game design.

Featured Project

Climb

"Climb" is an 2D vertical platformer that challenges players to ascend through treacherous rock formations with timing and strategic movement. Using simple three-button control system, players must carefully navigate between platforms while avoiding obstacles and little rest time. The game features a unique mechanic where players can traverse both left and right, adding a tactical layer to the climbing experience. Usage of the punch mechanism to clear obstacles and collection of power-ups are helpful for achieving higher scores and mastering the climb.
